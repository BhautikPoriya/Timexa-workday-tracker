## 📖 Overview

**Timexa** is a simple yet powerful workday tracker that calculates your effective working hours, break time, productivity, and expected clock-out time.

Just enter your punch-in and punch-out times, and Timexa automatically determines:

- ✅ Total work hours
- ☕ Total break time
- ⏳ Remaining hours to reach your goal
- 🚪 Estimated clock-out time
- 📈 Daily efficiency
- 📊 Live progress towards your work goal

The application runs **100% inside your browser** without requiring any backend, database, or login.

# 💻 How to Use

1. Open **index.html** in your browser.
2. Enter your punch times (one per line).
3. Or click **Punch Now** to add the current time.
4. Select your daily work goal.
5. View live calculations instantly.
